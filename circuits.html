Circuits

Selected circuits projects focused on hands-on measurement and simulation workflows — building, debugging, and validating analog and mixed-signal systems with real instrumentation.

Highlighted Projects

Op-Amps & Audio Signals (Multi-Stage Audio Chain)

Designed and built an end-to-end audio chain including input conditioning, active filtering, and a power amplifier capable of driving a low-impedance speaker load. Performance was validated stage-by-stage using oscilloscope measurements.

Op-Amps AC Coupling Active Filters Breadboard Oscilloscope
Multi-stage amplifier chain constructed on breadboard with input conditioning, filtering, and output stage driving a speaker.
Tektronix oscilloscope capture verifying expected gain and signal behavior across stages.

R-2R DAC + SAR ADC (Arduino + Comparator)

Implemented a 4-bit R-2R ladder DAC and paired it with a SAR-style ADC loop using a comparator and Arduino GPIO control/readback. Explored quantization behavior, thresholding, and mixed-signal integration through stepwise approximation.

R-2R Ladder Comparator Arduino GPIO Mixed-Signal
Breadboard implementation of a 4-bit R-2R ladder DAC and SAR ADC loop using Arduino GPIO control and a comparator.
Oscilloscope capture showing sampled/quantized behavior during successive approximation and DAC output settling.

RLC Step Response (Under / Critical / Over Damping)

Characterized RLC transient behavior by sweeping resistance values and comparing predicted vs. measured step responses. Related waveform shape to damping ratio and system parameters.

RLC Transients LTspice Scope Measurement System Damping
LTspice simulation illustrating underdamped, critically damped, and overdamped regimes.
Analytical derivation of natural frequency (ω₀), damping ratio (ζ), and regime classification.
