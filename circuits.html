Circuits

Selected circuits projects focused on hands-on measurement and simulation workflows -- building, debugging, and validating analog and mixed-signal systems with real instrumentation. Full details available upon request.

Highlighted Projects

Op-Amps & Audio Signals (Multi-Stage Audio Chain)

Designed and built an end-to-end audio chain: input conditioning (AC coupling + tunable gain), a higher-order filter stage, and an output amplifier capable of driving a speaker. Validated performance with scope-based measurements across stages.

Op-Amps AC Coupling Active/Passive Filters Breadboard + Scope
End-to-end amplifier chain with AC coupling, gain stage, and active filter driving a low-impedance speaker load. Circuit constructed and tuned on breadboard with stage-by-stage validation.
Measured stage responses on Tektronix oscilloscope to verify expected gain, filtering behavior, and signal integrity across the audio chain.

R-2R DAC + SAR ADC (Arduino + Comparator)

Implemented a 4-bit R-2R ladder DAC and paired it with a SAR-style ADC loop using a comparator and Arduino GPIO control/readback. Explored quantization behavior, thresholding, and mixed-signal integration through stepwise approximation.

R-2R Ladder Comparator Arduino GPIO Mixed-Signal

RLC Step Response (Under/Critical/Over Damping)

Characterized RLC transient response by sweeping resistance values and comparing predicted vs. measured step responses. Used simulation and scope captures to identify damping regimes and relate waveform shape to system parameters.

RLC Transients LTspice Scope Measurement System Damping
LTspice simulation of step response across varying resistance values, illustrating underdamped, critically damped, and overdamped regimes.
Analytical derivation of ω₀, damping ratio ζ, and classification of regimes.
